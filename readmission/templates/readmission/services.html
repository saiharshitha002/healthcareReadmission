Frequently Asked Questioins

These are the most frequently asked questions, and we have volunteered to provide answers to them without waiting for you to ask. If you have any queries, please feel free to post them.

  • Is my personal and medical information kept confidential and secure?

    Yes, your personal and medical information is kept confidential and secure. Healthcare providers and organizations have strict measures in place to protect your data and ensure that only authorized personnel can access it. Additionally, they have privacy policies that outline how they handle your data responsibly.

  • How do I schedule an online consultation with a healthcare professional?

    To schedule an online consultation with a healthcare professional, follow these simple steps:
    1. Click on "Make an Appointment" located on the home page, and it will redirect you to the scheduling page.

    2. Provide your details and select a date for your appointment.

    3. Choose the specific department you require and select a doctor.

    4. You can also include an optional message if needed.

    5. Click on "Make an Appointment" to finalize and schedule your consultation.

    6. Ensure you have a stable internet connection and find a quiet space for the consultation.

    • How can I update my personal information or account settings?

    To update your personal information or account settings, follow these steps:
    1. Log in to the website using your Gmail and password.

    2. Navigate to the "Settings" option, where you can access your personal details.

    3. Click on the "Edit" option to make any necessary changes.

    4. Remember to click the "Update" button after you have completed your edits.

    5. Once you've updated the details, you will receive a message saying "Successfully updated."

  • Are there any costs associated with using specific services on the website?

    No, there are no costs associated with using specific services on the website.

  • Are the healthcare providers listed on this website verified and licensed?

    Yes, the healthcare providers listed on this website are verified and licensed. They are experienced experts in their fields, ensuring you receive quality care and reliable medical advice.

  • Can I trust the information provided in the articles and resources?

    Yes, you can trust the information provided in the articles and resources on this website. The content is carefully reviewed by healthcare experts to ensure accuracy and reliability. The website is committed to providing credible and up-to-date information to help you make informed decisions about your health.
